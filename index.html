Frequently Asked Questions

Prompt engineering is the practice of crafting effective inputs for AI models to get desired outputs. It involves understanding how to structure instructions, provide context, and use specific techniques to improve AI responses.

Zero-shot prompting gives the AI a task without examples, relying on its training. Few-shot prompting provides one or more examples of the desired input-output format, helping guide the AI's response pattern.

Focus on clarity, specificity, and structure. Include role definitions, clear objectives, relevant context, output format specifications, and constraints. Test and iterate based on results.
